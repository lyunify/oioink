Our Story

OiOink (pronounced as "oink oink"‚Äîthe first "nk" is not pronounced when saying OiOink) is the sound of a piggy, and it represents something special: a piggy bank where children can safely learn about money. Most children don't get to practice with real money until they're much older. But why should that stop them from learning how to manage it? That's the challenge we wanted to solve. We asked ourselves: What if every child could learn financial skills in a safe space, regardless of their family's circumstances?

Our Vision

OiOink was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves the opportunity to learn about money, regardless of their circumstances. Many children don't have access to real money to practice with, but that shouldn't prevent them from building essential financial skills.

We've created a safe, virtual environment where children can explore financial concepts, practice making decisions, and build confidence‚Äîall without real-world financial risks. Through interactive lessons, virtual wallets, saving goals, and achievement systems, kids learn by doing, not just by reading.

Empowerment Through Design

We built OiOink because we got tired of seeing educational apps that tell kids exactly what to do. Instead, we wanted to create something that gives children real choices and lets them learn from their decisions. Every feature in OiOink puts kids in the driver's seat‚Äîthey choose their goals, make their own spending decisions, and see the results of their choices.

That's why OiOink teaches more than just how money works. It teaches kids to trust their judgment, think through problems, and feel confident making decisions. When children set their own saving goals, track their progress, and celebrate their achievements, they're building something bigger than financial knowledge‚Äîthey're building confidence in themselves.

For Every Child

OiOink is designed for children aged 6-12, regardless of their background or access to real money. Whether a child comes from a family that talks about money regularly, or one where financial topics aren't discussed, OiOink provides equal opportunities to learn and practice.
